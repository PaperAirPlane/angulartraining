Scope Levels

Conduct testing from big to small changes.

The most effective way of getting results from your split testings is to go from big (macro) to small (micro) changes. You conduct split testing for big changes to find the right direction and then pinpoint the exact optimal version with micro changes. In other words test big changes to find the global maxima and then test micro changes to pinpoint the local maxima.
